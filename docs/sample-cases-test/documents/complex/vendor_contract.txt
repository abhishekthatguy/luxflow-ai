SCOPE OF SERVICES
CloudSecure shall provide managed security services including:
  (a) 24/7 SOC monitoring and alert triage
  (b) Firewall and VPN administration (Palo Alto GlobalProtect)
  (c) Patch management for edge security devices (monthly maintenance windows)
  (d) Quarterly vulnerability assessments
  (e) Incident response support (first responder, not primary IR firm)

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
- Critical alert response: 15 minutes (24/7/365)
- VPN/firewall patch deployment: Within 14 business days of vendor patch release
- Uptime for managed devices: 99.9% monthly
- SLA credit: 5% monthly fee reduction per missed critical response; max 25% per month

FEES
Monthly retainer: $28,500 USD
Incident response overage: $275/hour beyond 40 included IR hours annually

BREACH NOTIFICATION OBLIGATIONS (Section 9.4)
CloudSecure shall notify Acme within twenty-four (24) hours of discovering:
  (i) Any unauthorized access to Acme systems under CloudSecure management
  (ii) Any confirmed or suspected data breach involving Acme customer data
  (iii) Any failure to meet SLA thresholds that could impact security posture

Notification shall include: nature of incident, affected systems, preliminary root cause,
and remediation steps. CloudSecure shall preserve all relevant logs for minimum 90 days.
