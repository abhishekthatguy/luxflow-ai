SECTION 4 — TIME OFF AND LEAVE

4.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 15 days PTO per year from hire date. Usable after
90 days. Manager approval required 5 business days in advance except emergencies.

4.2 PTO Carryover and Forfeiture
Unused PTO may carry over up to 5 days maximum. Balances exceeding 5 days on
December 31 are forfeited without compensation. Track balances in Workday.

SECTION 7 — PERFORMANCE

7.1 Performance Standards
Employees must meet role-specific goals, deadlines, and quality standards.

7.2 Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
Substandard performance may trigger a PIP with deficiencies, measurable goals,
and 30–60 day review. Failure to meet PIP goals may result in termination.

SECTION 8 — DISCIPLINE AND TERMINATION

8.1 Progressive Discipline
Generally: (1) Verbal counseling, (2) Written warning, (3) PIP or final warning,
(4) Termination. Serious misconduct may bypass steps.

8.2 Termination Procedures
HR and department leadership authorize terminations. Written notice states effective
date and general reason. Final pay per California Labor Code. Return company
property within 3 business days.

8.3 At-Will Employment
Employment is at-will unless stated otherwise in a signed agreement. This Handbook
does not create a contract or alter at-will status. Policies may change at any time.
